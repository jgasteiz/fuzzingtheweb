About Fuzzopress

Fuzzopress is a simple blogging app developed with Django. Its name comes from my personal blog name, Fuzzing the Web and from Octopress, another blogging app in which I've been inspired while doing the sidebar and other stuff.

You can take a look at the code on my github, download it, fork it... It has one of those do-whatever-you-want-but-don't-blame-me-if-it-crashes licenses :P
